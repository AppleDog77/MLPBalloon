--MLPB Readme--
Created by Odie at appledog77.github.io and ladybug-lane.neocities.org
My first ever balloon! Welcome to the MLP balloon! Meant to be used with my ghost Pinkie Pie and Rainbow Dash, but you can use it for anything else! In fact, it's public domain :D (Except, of course, for the background cutie mark graphics by BlackGryph0n; those are traced from the original MLPFiM show).

Contact: sleepysloth.turtle025@silomails.com

This balloon is redistributable, but don't delete anything! You can fix minor errors (typos, etc.), but be sure to add a notice that you did so in this Readme. You don't have to give me credit, but if you want to, just put this somewhere in the Readme, or the page where you have it for download: Created by Odie Dog at Ladybug Lane (https://ladybug-lane.neocities.org/). You can copy it as a template to make your own balloon for some reason, though, instead of the amazing Simplicity Balloon (https://ukagaka.zichqec.com/template/simplicity_balloon) by Zichqec, or the awesome GT Template (http://www.ashido.com/ukagaka/walkthrough.html) by Zarla, which I recommend you use instead. :P You can also study the code; I highly encourage it! I also welcome fanwork of my ukagaka-related stuff of any kind, I love it! :D
